National Integration camp 2023-24

The National Integration Camp (NIC) is organized every year and the duration of each camp is of 7 days with day-night boarding & lodging. These camps are held in different parts of the country. Each camp involves 200 NSS volunteers to undertake the scheduled activities. The primary objective of the camp is to make the NSS volunteers aware of the rich cultural diversity of India, history of our diversified culture, and national pride through knowledge about India
